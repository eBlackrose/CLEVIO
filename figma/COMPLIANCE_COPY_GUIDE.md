# CLEVIO Compliance Copy Guidelines

## Legal Requirements Overview

CLEVIO must carefully position its services to avoid:
1. **Implied AMEX endorsement** - No partnership or affiliation claims
2. **Guaranteed rewards** - Cannot promise specific AMEX benefits
3. **Banking claims** - Must clarify we're not a bank
4. **Investment management** - Must distinguish advisory from execution
5. **Absolute licensing claims** - Must be accurate and verifiable

---

## Critical Compliance Rules

### 1. American Express Relationship

**RULE**: CLEVIO has NO partnership, affiliation, or endorsement from American Express. All references must make this clear.

#### ✅ APPROVED Language

```
"Process payroll payments using your American Express business card"
"Enable payroll processing through American Express cards"
"Payroll payments are processed using an American Express card"
```

**Why it works**: Describes the technical function without implying partnership.

#### ❌ PROHIBITED Language

```
"AMEX-powered payroll" → Implies partnership
"Partnered with American Express" → False claim
"Official AMEX payroll provider" → Implies endorsement
"Powered by American Express" → Suggests affiliation
```

**Legal risk**: American Express trademark infringement, implied endorsement claim.

---

### 2. Rewards & Benefits

**RULE**: CLEVIO cannot guarantee, promise, or control AMEX rewards. All reward outcomes are determined by American Express.

#### ✅ APPROVED Language

```
"Potentially earn rewards based on your card agreement"
"Eligible cardholders may earn rewards according to AMEX terms"
"Rewards eligibility is determined solely by American Express"
```

**Required Disclaimer**:
> *Rewards eligibility, earning rates, and benefits are determined solely by American Express based on your specific card agreement. CLEVIO does not guarantee any particular reward earnings.*

#### ❌ PROHIBITED Language

```
"Earn AMEX rewards on every payroll" → Guarantees rewards
"Turn payroll into points" → Promises specific outcome
"Get rewarded for payroll" → Implies guarantee
"Maximize your AMEX rewards" → Overpromises control
"Unlock Centurion benefits" → False promise
```

**Legal risk**: False advertising, deceptive trade practices, FTC violation.

---

### 3. Banking Services

**RULE**: CLEVIO is NOT a bank and must never represent itself as one.

#### ✅ APPROVED Language

```
"CLEVIO is a financial services platform, not a bank"
"Banking services are provided by partner financial institutions, Members FDIC"
"Payment processing through partner banks"
```

**Required Disclosure** (Footer, Every Page):
> CLEVIO is not a bank. Banking services provided by partner institutions, Members FDIC.

#### ❌ PROHIBITED Language

```
"CLEVIO banking services" → Misrepresents status
"Your CLEVIO bank account" → Implies banking license
"Bank with CLEVIO" → False claim
"CLEVIO deposits" → Suggests deposit-taking
```

**Legal risk**: Regulatory violation, unlicensed banking activity claim.

---

### 4. Tax Services & Jurisdictional Limits

**RULE**: Tax services are subject to professional licensing in specific jurisdictions. Cannot claim universal coverage.

#### ✅ APPROVED Language

```
"Tax services provided by licensed CPAs and Enrolled Agents maintaining active credentials in applicable jurisdictions"
"Your assigned CPA holds appropriate state licenses for services provided"
"Tax professionals licensed where services are rendered"
```

**Required Disclosure** (Tax service activation):
> Tax services are subject to professional licensing in applicable jurisdictions. Your assigned CPA or EA will hold appropriate state licenses for the jurisdictions where services are provided.

#### ❌ PROHIBITED Language

```
"CPAs licensed in all 50 states" → May not be accurate for every advisor
"Nationwide tax services" → Overstates coverage
"Expert tax preparation anywhere in the US" → Jurisdictional overreach
```

**Legal risk**: Unauthorized practice of accounting, licensing violations.

---

### 5. Advisory Services Scope

**RULE**: Must distinguish between providing advice (allowed) and managing/executing (requires different licensing).

#### ✅ APPROVED Language

```
"Financial advisory services include strategic planning consultation and recommendations"
"Advisors provide guidance; you retain discretion over implementation"
"Licensed professionals offer advice based on your specific situation"
```

**Required Disclosure** (Advisory service):
> Financial advisory services include strategic planning consultation and recommendations. CLEVIO does not manage investment portfolios, execute trades, or provide insurance products. Implementation of recommendations is your responsibility.

#### ❌ PROHIBITED Language

```
"We manage your business finances" → Implies execution control
"CLEVIO handles your investments" → False scope claim
"Professional wealth management" → Implies investment management
"We'll optimize your portfolio" → Suggests execution
```

**Legal risk**: Unlicensed investment management, fiduciary breach.

---

### 6. Professional Credentials & Insurance

**RULE**: All claims about licensing and insurance must be accurate and verifiable.

#### ✅ APPROVED Language

```
"$5,000,000 professional liability insurance coverage maintained for advisory services"
"Licensed CPAs maintaining active credentials and continuing education"
"Professional liability coverage subject to policy terms and conditions"
```

**Required Disclosure** (Compliance page):
> Professional errors and omissions insurance with $5,000,000 coverage is maintained for tax and advisory services. Coverage applies to professional services rendered through CLEVIO and is subject to policy terms and conditions.

#### ❌ PROHIBITED Language

```
"Fully insured against all risks" → Overstates coverage
"Top-rated CPAs" → Unverifiable subjective claim
"Best advisors in the industry" → Unverifiable superlative
"Guaranteed outcomes with insurance protection" → Misrepresents insurance
```

**Legal risk**: Misrepresentation of coverage, deceptive advertising.

---

### 7. Security & Compliance Certifications

**RULE**: Certifications must be accurately represented with appropriate disclaimers.

#### ✅ APPROVED Language

```
"SOC 2 Type II certified security controls, independently audited annually"
"PCI DSS Level 1 compliant payment processing"
"Certification covers specified control objectives"
```

**Required Disclaimer** (Tooltip):
> SOC 2 Type II reports are available to customers under NDA. Certification covers specified control objectives and does not guarantee security against all threats.

#### ❌ PROHIBITED Language

```
"Unhackable security" → Impossible guarantee
"100% secure" → Absolute claim
"Guaranteed data protection" → Overpromises
```

**Legal risk**: False security claims, negligent misrepresentation.

by partner institutions, Members FDIC. Tax and advisory services provided by

Tax advisory services are provided by licensed Certified Public Accountants (CPAs)

CLEVIO is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by American Express. 
American Express is a registered trademark of American Express Company. Rewards, 
benefits, and card terms are determined solely by American Express and your specific 
card agreement. CLEVIO does not control, guarantee, or modify American Express 
earning rates, reward categories, or card benefits.

## Copy Review Checklist

Before publishing ANY copy, verify:

### AMEX-Related Copy
- [ ] Does NOT imply partnership, affiliation, or endorsement
- [ ] Does NOT guarantee specific rewards or benefits
- [ ] Includes appropriate reward disclaimer if discussing rewards
- [ ] Uses "American Express" (not "AMEX" alone in formal contexts)
- [ ] Includes trademark symbol if using "American Express" as product name
- [ ] States rewards are determined "solely by American Express"

### Banking & Financial Services
- [ ] Does NOT claim CLEVIO is a bank
- [ ] Clearly states partner banks provide banking services
- [ ] Includes "Members FDIC" when discussing deposit accounts
- [ ] Does NOT imply CLEVIO holds, manages, or controls funds

### Professional Services
- [ ] Distinguishes between advice and execution
- [ ] States implementation is customer's discretion
- [ ] Accurately represents licensing (with jurisdictional limits)
- [ ] Includes appropriate insurance disclaimers
- [ ] Does NOT make superlative claims ("best", "top-rated")

### Outcome Promises
- [ ] Does NOT guarantee tax savings
- [ ] Does NOT guarantee financial results
- [ ] Does NOT guarantee rewards earnings
- [ ] Does NOT promise specific outcomes

### Required Disclosures
- [ ] Footer disclaimer present on page
- [ ] Tooltips include appropriate disclaimers
- [ ] Subscription confirmations include all required acknowledgments
- [ ] Pricing examples include calculation disclaimers
